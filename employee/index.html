our mission

Human Capital been one of the key determinants of economic growth for both developing and advanced economies, then it is imperative to consider the major human capital development determinants which is EDUCATION/TRAINING. There exists an enormous interest in clarification of the relationship between education and economic growth. Over past 30 years, there have been conducted studies by economists about the connection between education and economic growth. There are actually many publications which provides strong evidence that suggest a correlation between the two. Applied Education has an exponential factor on economy of any nation. The exponential factor can either be positive or negative as the absence of entrepreneurial education result in poor economy. Community ResearchGate (CR) 2017 reports that 80% of graduates in Africa are unemployed and the impact of high unemployment index on the continent/national’s economy is unthinkable. The increase in crime, insecurity, suicide, poverty ratio, high mortality and many other socio-economic issues are resultant of large unproductive percentage of Africa countries. Even the 20% employed are low income earners which calls for serious attention and review. Currently the minimum wage across Africa is unthinkable compared to the rest of the world. One of the critical factor for abysmal minimum wage is the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) index of a nation and this could influence Gross National Income (GNI) of a nation upwardly or downwardly. The quality of education is one of the major factor for non-employability of most graduates in Africa and non-integration of entrepreneurial education in the course of study is limiting creativity, job creation, indigenous solution and execution of great idea of African Citizens. Sustainable Development Institute was establish to respond to this gaps and serves as knowledge incubation centre through training and development of her students towards the transformation of African nations.

A holistic institute committed to human capital development for sustainable growth.

We exist to enhance Human Capital Development through training with applied and Entrepreneurial Education, transforming Human, Community and Governance with sustainable development initiatives.

Our strategy
ENGAGE, EQUIP AND ENTRUST

We engage, equip and entrust students with cutting edge resources and training for personal, corporate, national and continental transformation. Trained students are in turn entrusted with transformation of their core platform of influence, community, nation and continent at large.

Our identity

Sustainable Development Institute is a faith based institute with sole aim of facilitating human capital development towards the transformation of human and community development systems through strategic resourcing and deployment of resourced for global educational, political and Economic advancement.

the company's background

Living in an era of Globalization, community forces are still perceived as the most supportive or strengthened factors to mobilize internal and external resources, and initiate development process for ensuring quality of life for those who live in all setting through a bottom-up approach. There is a broad agreement that community organization (formal or informal) play important role in mastering and fostering development initiatives by bringing in key actors in economy, leadership and social life of the community into development opportunity and facilitation. In pursuing its vision and mandate, Centre for Human and Community Development (CHCD) developed Community Driven Development Initiatives (CDDI) also known as Community Development and Discipleship Initiative, which are instrumental for people and National development. Development is achieved, and becomes genuine when it is driven by the Nationals through initiatives that are aimed at transforming human and national socio-economic life. These initiatives produce highly specialized public goods like improved human and social capital of a Nation. Sustainable Development Institute exist to provide educational services through a cutting edge and specific training towards human capital development for a sustainable and balanced national growth. Specific education is very important for strategic growth and development of all citizens for productive contribution in Education, Health, and Economic and Political life of any nation. SDI engage, equip and entrust classified ignorant or uneducated and other marginalized/disadvantaged individuals with capacity needed for their inherent potential development. The uneducated are often described as reason for poverty of any nation. In contrast, SDI considers the uneducated and their ignorant state as an assets, and productive citizens in development process as we engage, equip and entrust such with specific training for effective service delivery that aids or leads to personal and national economic growth and transformation.

OUR AIMS/OBJECTIVES.

The aims of Sustainable Development Institute is to enable citizens realize their full potentials through self-realization and development of necessary skills for effective execution of ideas with idea incubation, strategic thinking, planning and implementation. The institute mark qualitative and quantitative growth of her student for strategic development and deployment. The Institute aims at promoting education, research, specific training and professional development with specific attention to productive and non-productive echelon of society as we improve socio-economic and spiritual life of all citizens.

  • To organize educational and professional courses
  • To work as training agency for personnel’s, corporate organizations and government
  • To conduct research and develop strategy for solving specific problem
  • To organize seminars, conferences, study groups, lectures, and social action for transformation
  • To undertake monitoring and evaluation studies
  • To provide consultancy service in the fields of training, research and development
  • To initiate and facilitate innovative development projects
  • To initiate and facilitate Networking platforms for professionals and resource development
  • To prepare and provide print, audio-visual and other resource materials for training, education, Health, Socio-economic and research activities
  • To train and mobilize local personnel, resources in employment and income generating activities for students
